Customer Reviews Dashboard

  • Category: Tableau Dashboard
  • Client: Axon Enterprises
  • Project date: May, 2023
  • Project URL: Axon Dashboard

Summary

  • Axon Enterprise, Inc. utilizes Net Promoter Score (NPS) as a top-level metric to measure customer satisfaction and prioritize customer needs. While the company has a high percentage of Promoters, the high percentage of detractors in 2022, particularly in May, highlights the need to focus on improving product and customer service to meet customer expectations and increase satisfaction. Categorizing Promoters and accurately diagnosing areas of improvement will enhance the NPS score, attract new customers, and improve customer retention.

  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Net Promoter Score (NPS), Percentage of Promoters, Percentage of Detractors, Number of Detractors in specific periods.

  • The findings revealed that the company has a high percentage of detractors, particularly in May 2022. This indicates potential areas for improvement in product and customer service. The high percentage of Promoters provides an opportunity to leverage positive feedback for attracting new customers and improving customer retention.

  • Recommendations: To improve customer experience and NPS score, Axon should focus on addressing the issues raised by detractors and implementing changes based on customer feedback. Categorizing Promoters will allow for a more accurate diagnosis of areas that require improvement. By proactively addressing customer needs, the company can enhance overall satisfaction, attract new customers, and increase customer loyalty.
